This is a Pufferfish

Pufferfish, belonging to the Tetraodontidae family, are unique marine creatures known for their distinctive ability to inflate their bodies by taking in water or air, serving as a defense mechanism against predators. They are found in various marine environments and are notable for their intricate behaviors and intricate physiological adaptations.
